TDB is a system architecture direction for running data workloads on tensor runtimes and accelerators.

## Scope

- SQL-style relational operators on tensor backends.
- Graph query and analytics support under a unified execution substrate.
- Accelerator-aware optimization for GPU/NPU environments.

## Why It Matters

As AI and analytics stacks converge, systems need high-throughput execution that can operate consistently across structured and unstructured data operations.

## Current Emphasis

- Unified operator abstraction for SQL and graph workloads.
- Runtime efficiency under heterogeneous hardware.
- Composability with AI-oriented data processing pipelines.
